NASA's NEOWISE Infrared Heritage will live on


After 14 successful years in space, increased solar activity is causing the NEOWISE mission to end on July 31. NEO Surveyor, NASA’s next-generation infrared space telescope dedicated to hunting hazardous near-Earth objects, is set for launch in late 2027. NEO Surveyor seeks the most difficult-to-find asteroids and comets that could damage Earth.

NASA's New Discovery: Mars Rover Perseverance's Latest Find


NASA’s Perseverance rover has discovered intriguing rock formations on Mars that could reveal clues about the planet’s past habitability. These findings could advance our understanding of Mars and the potential for past microbial life.

Hubble Space Telescope Captures Stunning Nebula Image


The Hubble Space Telescope has captured an awe-inspiring image of the Eagle Nebula, showcasing the intricate beauty of cosmic dust and star formation. This new image provides a deeper understanding of stellar nurseries and the lifecycle of stars.
